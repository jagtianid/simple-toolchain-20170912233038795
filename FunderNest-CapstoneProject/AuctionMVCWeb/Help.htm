How to use the Auction

Review the item: Make sure you know what you are buying. Once you find a listing that interests you, click on it to view details of the item and find out as much as you can. Once you've found the item you want to bid for decide the maximum you're willing to pay.

Place A Bid: Enter the price you are prepared to pay for the item. Keep watching to track the bidding progress of the item you have bid for.

Paying for your item: After you have won the item, please pay cash to the Auction representative Mr X ext 1234.
